Hello, this is Hyerin Kim’s web site. I will talk about a city where I’ve lived.

The city is Song-Do, and it's in South Korea. Actually, Song-Do is a reclaimed ground. It's modern and the air is cleaner than other cities. And this city gives exotic appearance. Because many Korean cities have old apartments and residences, but Song-Do isn't. As shown in the picture below, it is the city where has the many latest skyscrapers. Even, the nature is placed around the buildings. It looks like a Central Park in NY, so most of Korean call Song-Do to Korean Central Park. If you have a chance to visit Korea, I recommend you should drop in Song-Do.

The first picture is night view in Song-Do. At night, the lights on buildings and the street lamps are turned, so we can see the bright and colorful night.

On the other hands, the second one is the landscape in the daytime. If you want to see the whole site of Song-Do, you can see the view at the top floor of the highest building. Because Song-Do doesn't have any mountain, so it's flat.
